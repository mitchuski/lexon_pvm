- **cityofmages was held, deliberately.** Its uncommitted tree is a broad
  multi-session batch (Tome IX drafts 6-8, Tome X Act 2, a grimoire patch,
  hearthold) that is not this session's work and has not been through First
  -Person review. Act 9 rides that review batch rather than being pushed
  piecemeal; it is already carried in the guide snapshot, so it publishes
  through the static site the way Acts 6-8 did before it.
- **The register was not written.** The register is operator-write-only; the
  candidates wait unnumbered in the "Incoming" section's sense, and the
  numbers and confidences are the First Person's. No C-number was assigned.
- **The model JSON and the Programme master were not edited.** The Programme
  allusions are the First Person's incremental flow; this arc prepared the
  substance those allusions point to, and left the master documents alone.
- **A push ≠ a deploy.** The guide repo is pushed; `guide.agentprivacy.ai`
  goes live on the manual Cloudflare step.
