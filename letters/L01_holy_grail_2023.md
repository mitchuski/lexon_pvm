Open question to carry forward: log loss risk versus public chain persistence, and whether VRC redundancy solves it.

Compare with three-axis separation: Φ_agent, Φ_data, Φ_inference as multiplicative, collapse-on-any-axis. That tension is a finding, not a bug.
